**Bolivars to Dollars** is a currency conversion application that displays exchange rates between Venezuelan bolívares (VES) and US dollars (USD), euros (EUR), and USDT (P2P market reference).

### Data Sources

The app displays exchange rates obtained from:
- **DolarApi.com** - BCV official rates for USD/VES and EUR/VES
- **Binance P2P / DolarApi.com** - P2P market reference for USDT/VES

### Important Disclaimer

This application is **not an exchange platform** and does **not facilitate transactions** or **set exchange rates**. It only displays publicly available information for informational purposes only.

**The only official exchange rate in Venezuela is the one published by the Central Bank of Venezuela (BCV).**
